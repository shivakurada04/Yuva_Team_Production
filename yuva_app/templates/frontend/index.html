Who We Are

Yuva Team is a social service organization founded in 2009 by Sudhagani Raju and a passionate group of friends united by a common mission to create a positive impact in society. What began as a team of 100+ big-hearted volunteers has now grown into a vibrant, community-driven organization dedicated to supporting education, empowering youth, and uplifting underprivileged communities.
