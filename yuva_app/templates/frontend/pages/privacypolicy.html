Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect the personal information of individuals who visit our website or participate in our programs.

1. Information Collection

We may collect personal information such as name, email address, phone number, and donation details when you:

  • Sign up for newsletters or updates
  • Make donations or contributions
  • Contact us for inquiries

2. Use of Information

The information collected is used solely to:

  • Process donations and funding
  • Communicate important updates and information
  • Improve our programs and services
  • Comply with legal and regulatory requirements

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade personal information to third parties. Information may only be shared with trusted partners directly involved in program implementation, and strictly for legitimate purposes.

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect personal data against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

5. Cookies

Our website may use cookies to enhance user experience. Cookies are small data files stored on your device to help us analyze web traffic and improve site functionality.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of your personal information at any time by contacting us at yuvateam2009@gmail.com

7. Changes to this Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
