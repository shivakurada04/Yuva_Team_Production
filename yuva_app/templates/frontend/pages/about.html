About Us

Overview

Yuva is a non-profit social organization founded in 2009 by a group of passionate individuals who shared a common dream — to create a positive difference in society. What started as a small team with big hearts has grown into a community-driven organization dedicated to supporting education, empowering youth, and helping people in need.

Educational Support

Education is the foundation of growth, and we believe no child should be left behind because of financial struggles. Yuva identifies students from underprivileged backgrounds and provides them with essential learning materials such as notebooks, textbooks, exam pads, pens, and school bags.

We also support children who face difficulties in paying school or college fees by offering funding assistance through contributions from well-wishers and community donors.

Funding Assistance

Many young students with talent and determination are forced to discontinue their education due to a lack of financial support. Yuva steps in to bridge that gap. Through our funding programs, we sponsor school and tuition fees, help with exam registration costs, and provide educational tools or uniforms when needed.

Community Help & Welfare

Yuva works actively to support families in need by providing daily essentials, clothes, groceries, and hygiene materials. We organize donation drives and distribute basic necessities to low-income households, elderly individuals, and people facing hardship.

Health & COVID-19 Relief

Health is a vital part of community well-being, and Yuva has consistently taken steps to promote health awareness and support. During the COVID-19 pandemic, our team worked tirelessly to provide food packets, masks, sanitizers, and medicines to affected families and frontline workers.

Social and Environmental Work

Yuva believes that protecting the environment is as important as uplifting people. We regularly conduct clean-up campaigns, tree plantation drives, and environmental awareness events in schools and public places.

Our Vision

To create a society where every person, especially the youth, has the strength, opportunity, and motivation to shape a better and more inclusive future. We strive for a world where young minds are inspired to dream, guided to act, and supported to overcome challenges, ensuring equality, dignity, and hope for all.

Our Story

The journey of Yuva began when a small group of friends decided to take action instead of waiting for change. They started by helping students who couldn’t afford school essentials and later extended their efforts to broader social causes.

Our Impact

Yuva has reached hundreds of students and families through its initiatives — spreading hope, kindness, and opportunity where it’s needed most.
