About YUVA

Empowering students through education, care, and community support

Get In Touch

Overview

Yuva is a non-profit social organization founded in 2009 by a group of passionate individuals who shared a common dream — to create a positive difference in society. What started as a small team with big hearts has grown into a community-driven organization dedicated to supporting education, empowering youth, and helping people in need.

Education & Support

Providing students with notebooks, textbooks, pens, and school bags. Supporting tuition, exam fees, and uniforms to ensure every child can continue learning.

Community Welfare

Helping families in need with groceries, clothes, and hygiene essentials. Volunteers visit communities to understand their needs and provide support where it’s most needed.

Health & Relief

Promoting health awareness and organizing health camps, blood donation drives, and emergency relief programs, including support during COVID-19.

Environment

Conducting clean-up drives, tree plantations, and awareness programs in schools and public spaces to encourage eco-friendly habits.

Our Vision

To create a society where every person, especially the youth, has the strength, opportunity, and motivation to shape a better and more inclusive future. We strive for a world where young minds are inspired to dream, guided to act, and supported to overcome challenges, ensuring equality, dignity, and hope for all.

Our Mission

To empower young minds and uplift communities by promoting education, equality, and social responsibility.We focus on providing quality educational opportunities, guidance, and resources to those in need.Through inclusive initiatives, we work to create lasting impact and a brighter future for all.

Our Story & Founder

Yuva began with a simple belief — change happens when people come together to help others. It started by supporting students who lacked basic learning resources. Over time, more volunteers joined and our work expanded to community support, health initiatives, and environmental care, guided by compassion and responsibility.

“I believe that education is the strongest foundation for meaningful change. Every initiative we undertake empowers young minds, supports underserved communities, and inspires hope.” — Founder Words

Sudhagani Raju

Founder & Convenor

Our Impact

Our impact goes beyond numbers — it’s seen in students gaining confidence, families receiving timely help, and communities coming together to support each other.

Education

Students continue learning without fear, supported with materials and financial assistance.

Community Welfare

Families gain access to essentials like food, clothing, and hygiene support during difficult times.

Health & Relief

Aid during emergencies, health awareness programs, and community support have strengthened wellbeing.

Environment

Our clean-up drives and tree planting initiatives create greener, safer, and healthier communities.

Our Team

Our strength lies in our team of passionate volunteers, coordinators, and youth leaders who dedicate their time and energy to serve society.
